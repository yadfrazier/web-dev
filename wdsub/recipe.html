How to Make a perfect Steak 中巧

  1. Ingredients

    1. A Nicley Marbled Steak of your choice 巧
    2. Seasoning's (such as salt ,pepper ,onion powder ,garlic powder etc.)
    3. A Stove top cooking surface
    4. Salted Butter
    5. Garlic Cloves
    6. Rosemary
    7. A Cooking Thermometer

  2. Preperation

    1. Pat your Steak down from any blood or moisture to have seasonings stick on well
    2. Poke holes in said Steak after you have dried it for the seasoning to be absored well, then aply seasonings
    3. Microwave a half stick of Butter and a Garlic clove for 30 seconds until you have a watery consistence
    4. Baste said Steak with the butter of Joy and Happiness, Make sure (Helps it not stick to the pan)
    5. Preheat stove top to setting 7 or 8

  3. Cooking Time / Methode

    1. Slap that Slab of meat into a pan with the rest of the butter juice中
    2. It is very important to baste the steak while flipping each side every 2 minutes 30 seconds
    3. After the 3 minute mark you can use a Thermometer to check the internal temperture

  4. Tips for Perfection

    • Not sure how you like your beautiful Steak? Press your thumb against any of your fingers on your left hand. Then touch the bottom inner part of your thumb whilst touching th other finger. Pinky To Thumb- Well Done, Ring to Thumb- Medium,Middle to thumb etc to change the "rarity"
    • When Brining make sure its never over 2 to 3 hours, anything over will make the steak salty
    • Season Before During And After Cooking, it naturally desolves salts,peppers etc. whilist being cooked
    • Serve with a bit of rosemary on top for taste and looks中
