Top 5 most heavy Metallica Albums💿

These albums are in order of release. These are my humble opinions of each of the albums

First Album: Kill Em' All

Our first album released in 1983 is KILL EM ALL . Kill Em all is a great first album for that thrashy 80's San Fransico Bay area sound the people wanted. This broke off of the "No life till Leather" demo released a bit earlier in 81 a couple years prior. Overall its a decent first ablum for the scene. Id say the best song off of this album is maybe Cliffs Bass solo of "Anesthia Pulling Teeth", its great for someone his age to put that effort into it. Not as thrashy for a beginning but very solid for a first album, 7.8/10

Second Album: Ride The Lightning

Now, we have what i personally think is the star of the show. Metallica's second studio almbum, RIDE THE LIGHTNING. Ride the lightning was released in 84 but recorded half way across the world in denmark since it was cheaper, but still very far from "Kill Em All's" original place of recording which is Syracuse, NY. This has some great meaterial the guys created from some fan thrash favorites like CREEPING DEATH and TRAPPED UNDER ICE , but along with the subtle balad FADE TO BLACK . Over all, 10/10

Third Album: Master Of Puppets

Released in 1986, Master of Puppets is metallica's 3rd studio album. The album Metallica is most known for from the catchy intro riff of MASTER OF PUPPETS , to the main title song being used in the show "Stranger Things". This album represents the bridge from that thrash/heavy metal to a full on thrash take over. In my humble opinion the best song off of this album has to be LEPER MESSIAH. This album is a great album for the band's starting large future carrer, 9.4/10

Fourth Album: And Justice For All...

And Justice For All... (AJFA) was released in 1988 proceeding cliffs death 2 years prior. This was metallicas first major project without the early bassist. Despite his death, many fans call this metallicas most techincal and thrash/hevay metal album to be put out by the band. It was a big surprise from Master of puppets. Dont get me wrong this album is extremley technical as i have tried to learn a couple of the songs but stopped halfway through. This album is absolutley outstanding but just not my cup of tea. Extremley well written, produced, and played live is the album "And Justice For All.... 9.2/10
